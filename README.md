# about-US-Iran-negotiations-
please read carefully 
In negotiations, both sides must show each other goodwill and concessions.
The United States must lift the economic blockade and harsh sanctions on Iran so that the Iranian people can breathe again and come to the negotiating table with dignity and full consent. Only when Iran is relieved from this suffocating pressure can real, honest, and productive talks begin.
Peace cannot be achieved through threats, sanctions, and bombs. True peace comes when both sides treat each other with respect. Iran has repeatedly shown its willingness to negotiate. Now it is time for the United States to show genuine goodwill by removing the maximum pressure campaign and economic siege.
We call on the American administration to:

Immediately lift the most painful economic sanctions
Stop the military threats and aggression
Return to serious diplomacy and negotiations
Work toward a comprehensive, fair, and permanent agreement that guarantees long-term peace

Iran, in return, is ready to offer real guarantees regarding its nuclear program and regional issues — but only in an atmosphere of mutual respect, not under the shadow of sanctions and war.
The Iranian people are exhausted by war and sanctions. The American people are also tired of endless conflicts in the Middle East. This is the moment for both nations to choose wisdom over pride, and peace over destruction.
End the blockade. End the war. End the suffering.
Let us seize this opportunity to write a new chapter — a chapter of dignity, stability, and lasting peace between Iran and the United States. The world is watching. Future generations will judge us by the choices we make today.
Peace is possible. Peace is necessary. Peace must come now.
